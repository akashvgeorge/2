About Alappuzha:

Alappuzha, also known as Alleppey, is a coastal town located in the southern Indian state of Kerala. It is situated around 150 km south of Kochi and 60 km north of Trivandrum. Alappuzha is famous for its beautiful backwaters, which are a network of interconnected canals, lagoons, and lakes that run parallel to the Arabian Sea. Visitors can take a boat ride on the backwaters and enjoy the serene beauty of the place. Alappuzha is also known for its houseboat cruises, which provide a unique experience of living on water and exploring the backwaters. The town is also home to many beautiful beaches, including Alappuzha Beach, Marari Beach, and Andhakaranazhi Beach. These beaches are ideal for relaxing, swimming, and sunbathing. Alappuzha is also a hub for the coir industry, and visitors can see how coir products are made and buy them as souvenirs. The town is also famous for its toddy shops, where visitors can try toddy, a local alcoholic beverage made from coconut palm sap. Overall, Alappuzha is a beautiful place that offers a unique mix of natural beauty, cultural experiences, and leisure activities
